About Us

Ever since we began our journey in the catering world more than three decades ago, our company has been transforming the wedding hospitality industry by efficiently introducing new services, but still following the finest of traditional catering methods. We took time and research and introduced new elements and services to the industry. We trained our chefs to be experts in both, authentic Indian and world cuisines. We have not only travelled but collaborated with top restaurants and chefs to get the taste of various cuisines just right. Our objective is to win the hearts of our clients with our warm hospitality and impeccable service, and be known as the catering company with sheer variety and flexibility of delectable dishes.

why us?

Our USP is "Excellence through quality food and service" and we make sure to provide our clients cwith value for money. When you work with Diamond Hospitality, the experience enlivens the senses and we fulfil even the unexpressed wishes and needs of our guests.

our beliefs

Our greatest asset, and the key to our success, is our people. Each person in our team takes pride in what he does and we work together to get the right results. We come with a reputation and do our best to live up to every bit of it.

our mission

We at Diamond Hospitality are focussed on fulfilling our mission, that is to be a trendsetter in the industry and most importantly, to provide each of our valued clients with exceptional and memorable experiences of their events.
